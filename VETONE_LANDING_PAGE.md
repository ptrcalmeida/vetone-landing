## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my patient data secure?
Yes. We use bank-level encryption, comply with LGPD, and never share your data with third parties. You maintain complete ownership of all patient information.

### Does it work offline?
Core features like patient records, consultation recording, and SOAP note generation work offline. AI analysis and research require an internet connection.

### Can I import existing patient records?
Yes. We support CSV imports and can help migrate data from common veterinary management systems.

### What languages are supported?
Currently optimized for Brazilian Portuguese with plans to expand to Spanish and English in 2025.

### How accurate is the AI diagnosis?
Our AI provides suggestions to support‚Äînot replace‚Äîyour clinical judgment. It has been trained on millions of veterinary cases and peer-reviewed literature, but you always make the final diagnostic and treatment decisions.

### Can multiple veterinarians share the same patient database?
Yes, with our Clinic and Enterprise plans. Each veterinarian has their own account with controlled access to shared patient records.

### What devices are compatible?
iPhone (iOS 14.0+), iPad, and Android phones/tablets (Android 7.0+). Minimum 2GB RAM recommended.

### How is pricing calculated?
Monthly per veterinarian for Professional Plan. Clinic Plan offers volume discounts. No hidden fees‚Äîincludes all updates and support.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings with no penalties.

### Do you offer training?
Yes. Professional Plan includes video tutorials. Clinic and Enterprise Plans include personalized onboarding and ongoing training support.
